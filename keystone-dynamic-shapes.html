Wide flared top + concave inward sweep. The most dynamic option — dramatic proportions with elegant curves.

Angular shoulders at top + concave sweep below. Structured crown with elegant taper. Best of both worlds.

Flared crown + angular shoulders + concave sweep. All three moves combined. Maximum dynamic energy while staying subtle.
